Parties embroiled in a dispute increasingly use mediation as their preferred tool for dispute resolution because it's quick, inexpensive, and lets them maintain control.

Lawyers looking for a neutral committed to settling even the most complicated disputes have trusted Chris Soelling for the last 27 years. He has mediated and arbitrated thousands of cases in the commercial arena, including construction, real estate, insurance coverage, and business disputes of all kinds. Chris is especially recognized for resolving challenging cases involving multiple parties.
